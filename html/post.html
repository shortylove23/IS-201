The Beginning

Oslo, previously known as Christiana was settled by vikings around the year 1000 AD. The first king to reign over this beautiful land was none other than Christian IV of Denmark. He came to the city and named it after his own. Since then, Oslo has become home to thousands and is known for it's vibrant culture, art, and food. I loved having the opportunity to serve there for 9 months of my mission.
